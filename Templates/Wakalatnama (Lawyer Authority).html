WAKALATNAMA

(Power of Attorney / Authority to Appear)

[Under the provisions of the Qanun-e-Shahadat Order 1984 & High Court Rules 2026]

I/We, the undersigned Client/Party/Parties, do hereby nominate, constitute, and appoint Mr./Ms. [Advocate’s Full Name], Advocate, High Court, as my/our true and lawful Attorney and Wakil, to act on my/our behalf in the matter of:

Case Title: _________________________________________
Court: Honourable High Court of _________________________________________
Jurisdiction: Appellate/Special Original/Constitutional jurisdiction.

1. Scope of Authority — Appellate & Original Powers

The said Advocate is hereby authorized to appear, act, and plead for me/us in the above matter, including but not limited to appellate courts, division benches, single benches, registrars, and all connected revision, review, or reference applications arising from any judgment or interim order.

2. Signing of Pleadings, Petitions & Documents

The Wakil shall have full power and authority to sign, verify, present, and file all pleadings, petitions, written statements, replies, rejoinders, applications (including for interim relief, injunction, or stay), and any other legal papers as may be deemed necessary for the proper conduct of the case before any forum.

3. Authority to Receive Payments & Settle Accounts

The Advocate is hereby empowered to receive on my/our behalf all payments, cheques, demand drafts, court-fee refunds, security deposits, and any monies awarded by the court, whether by way of decree, order, or settlement. Receipt given by the Advocate shall be a valid and binding discharge for the paying party/court.

4. Appointment of Associates & Junior Counsel

The Wakil is authorized to engage, instruct, and appoint one or more associate advocates, junior counsel, or legal assistants (including advocates-on-record of subordinate courts) to appear on my/our behalf, to sign papers in the Wakil’s absence, or to argue part or whole of the case, at the Wakil’s sole discretion and without separate notice to me/us.

  • The Principal Advocate shall remain primarily responsible for the conduct of the case.
  • Any fees payable to associates shall be borne by the Principal Advocate unless otherwise agreed in writing.

5. General Powers & Indemnity

I/We hereby ratify and confirm all acts lawfully done by the said Advocate under this Wakalatnama. The authority includes the power to withdraw or compromise the matter, but only upon express written consent from me/us (unless such power is specifically added in separate schedule).

This Wakalatnama shall remain in full force until final disposal of the matter including all appeals, executions, and review applications, unless earlier revoked by written notice to the court.
