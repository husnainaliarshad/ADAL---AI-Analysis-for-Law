6. TERMINATION & NOTICE PERIOD

Either party may terminate this tenancy by serving a written notice of ONE (1) CALENDAR MONTH to the other party [citation:3]. The notice period shall commence from the date of receipt of such notice. Upon expiry of the notice period, the Tenant shall vacate the premises and hand over vacant peaceful possession to the Landlord. Failure to vacate on time shall entitle the Landlord to charge double the monthly rent as damages for holdover, as per prevailing rent laws.

  • Landlord’s responsibility (major structural): Major plumbing, electrical wiring, water proofing of roof, structural repairs, and maintenance of building common areas (lifts, stairs, main entrance).
