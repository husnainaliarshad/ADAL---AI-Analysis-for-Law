About the Project

Built for the Pakistani legal mind.

FYP · Session 2022–2026

Our Vision

We envision a Pakistan where legal professionals — whether seasoned advocates or first-year law students — are never slowed down by the friction of manual research. ADAL aims to be the intelligent layer between raw legal data and actionable insight, making the law more accessible, accurate, and efficient for everyone who depends on it.

Our Mission

ADAL is built to automate the most time-consuming parts of legal work: sifting through judgments, verifying citations, and generating structured drafts. By combining retrieval-augmented generation, OCR, and natural language processing — trained and tuned for Pakistani case law — we are reducing hours of research to seconds of insight.

Why now?

The legal industry in Pakistan has been underserved by technology for too long. Existing tools are either unaffordable, built for foreign jurisdictions, or lack intelligent automation altogether. ADAL is a direct answer to that gap — purpose-built, locally-grounded, and powered by modern AI.

What We Offer

Every tool a legal professional needs.

05 services

01

Document Ingestion & OCR

Upload PDFs, Word documents, or scanned images — including Urdu-script material. ADAL digitizes and structures your documents automatically, no matter the quality of the scan.

02

Citation Verification

Automatically extract every legal citation from your document and validate it against a structured database of Pakistani judgments — with confidence scores for each reference found.

03

AI Summarization & Precedent Retrieval

Generate concise summaries of lengthy judgments and instantly retrieve semantically similar precedents — powered by RAG, not just keyword matching.

04

Legal Drafting Assistant

Describe your case and let ADAL generate structured legal drafts — contracts, notices, and applications — using your prompt and relevant case law as context for the LLM.

05

Search & Knowledge Graph

Search case law in plain language and explore how cases relate to each other through an interactive knowledge graph — go beyond keyword search to true semantic discovery.

06

Interactive Legal Assistant

Ask questions about your uploaded documents in plain English. The AI assistant retrieves and synthesizes relevant legal information, giving you answers grounded in real case law.
